Our Vision for the Next 6 Months

This document outlines our strategic roadmap to establish Bunkspace as a premier creative agency specializing in Wix Studio. We will focus on deep skill development, creating a powerful portfolio, and building an engaged community to drive our growth.

The 6-Month Roadmap

Our plan is structured into two focused phases. Explore the goals and milestones for each quarter.

Q3

Phase 1: Foundation & Mastery

July - September. This foundational quarter is dedicated to building our core skills, creating assets, and establishing our technical authority on the Wix Studio platform.

✓

Master Wix Studio: Achieve deep expertise through intensive learning and hands-on project application.

✓

Build Core Portfolio: Develop 5 high-quality websites to showcase our capabilities.

✓

Develop Sellable Components: Create custom components for the Wix Marketplace to generate revenue and build authority.

Q4

Phase 2: Growth & Launch

October - December. With our foundation set, this quarter focuses on strategic branding, market positioning, and preparing for client acquisition.

✓

Rebrand Bunkspace: Launch a new brand identity focused on our Wix Studio specialization.

✓

Showcase Full Portfolio: Feature 6 completed projects to demonstrate our range and skill.

✓

Achieve Wix Partner Status: Secure official recognition from Wix to validate our expertise and build trust.

Execution & Accountability

A great plan requires disciplined execution. Here is our framework for ensuring we stay on track and accountable to our goals.

A mandatory 1-2 hour meeting every Sunday to ensure constant alignment and momentum.

  • Agenda 2:Set clear, actionable goals for the upcoming week.
  • Agenda 3:Identify and collaboratively solve any blockers.
